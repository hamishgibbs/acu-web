About Me

Debra Lee provides integrative family health care, treating people throughout their life cycle. Her clients include youngsters, elders, and everyone in between. Acupuncture is an effective treatment for a wide variety of conditions including digestive issues, sleep disturbances, allergies and arthritis. In addition to a broad general practice, she has specialized training in the areas of emotional well being, pain relief, and women’s reproductive health. In order to make acupuncture accessible, Debra Lee incorporates a reasonable fee structure and is a provider with Excellus and Pomco Insurance. Individual insurance plans differ, so your plan's acupuncture benefits can be explored through Debra Lee’s billing service.
